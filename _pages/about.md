I am a third year PhD student at KAIST in [Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence (MLAI) Lab](https://www.mlai-kaist.com/), under [Prof. Sung Ju Hwang](http://www.sungjuhwang.com/). I have been working on the intersections of AI and life science, bridging their gap by modeling biomolecules and their complex system via representation learning, generative models, and LLMs. [[CV](https://DongkiKim95.github.io/files/_CV__2026_02_05.pdf)]
